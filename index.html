Project Overview:

Visually impaired students often face challenges in finding reliable scribes during exams. Our DTSI project aims to build a structured and sustainable system to connect volunteer scribes with blind students effectively and in a timely manner.

Key Objectives:

  • Create a centralized pool of verified volunteer scribes
  • Use a simple app or online platform for blind students to request scribes
  • Track volunteer contributions using a monthly leaderboard
  • Recognize top volunteers to maintain motivation and community engagement

Gamification Plan:

To encourage consistent volunteer participation, we will introduce a monthly leaderboard. Top 3 scribes will be recognized with certificates and small prizes, such as stationery kits, gift vouchers, or books by great authors.

Support Requested:

We kindly request monthly sponsorship support to help us offer prizes or recognition items to the top volunteers. Estimated cost: INR 500–1000 per month.
